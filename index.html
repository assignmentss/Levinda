Introduction

Lavinda Eye Specialist Hospital serves a large ophthalmology population but faces delays in patient flow, limited digital presence, equipment malfunctions, and budgetary constraints. The objective of this consultancy was to assess root causes and recommend practical solutions. Recommendations include: a digital appointment and patient flow management system, preventive maintenance protocols, and a hospital website with a digital marketing strategy.

Recommendation 1: Digital Appointment & Flow Management

The manual appointment system creates bottlenecks. Recommendation: adopt a digital appointment and AI-assisted patient flow system.

  • Streamlined operations and improved service delivery
  • Reduced waiting times (~54% improvement in studies)
  • Automated reminders to lower no-shows
  • Real-time visibility for clinics and staff

Recommendation 2: Preventive & Predictive Maintenance

Critical equipment malfunctions cause delays and rescheduling. Recommendation: implement a preventive maintenance program with schedules, checklists, and vendor SLAs.

  • Higher uptime and reliability (target ‚â•98%)
  • Reduced maintenance costs (12‚Äì18% lower)
  • Improved patient experience and fewer cancellations

Recommendation 3: Website & Digital Marketing

Lavinda currently lacks an active website and digital presence. Recommendation: launch a modern, mobile-first website with service pages, clinician profiles, booking features, and patient education content.

  • Launch website MVP with online booking
  • Create Google Business Profile & improve maps visibility
  • Apply basic SEO for discoverability
  • Provide timely patient engagement via messaging
